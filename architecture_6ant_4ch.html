Silicon to Software (S2S) · P1 Architecture Revision

6-Antenna × 4-Channel Independent RF Chains

Each receiver antenna drives a fully independent RF chain. After the per-antenna LNA, the signal fans out into its own 4-channel channelised filter bank — no shared splitter, no pre-channelisation combining. Spatial (AoA / DF) information is preserved end-to-end for radar / EW use.
6 Antennas 6 Independent Chains 6× SAW Preselectors 4 Channels per Chain 24 RF Outputs No Shared Combiner
Topology rule 1No RF combining between antennas before channelisation.
Topology rule 2Each antenna owns its SMA → Limiter → Bias-T → LNA → 4-way splitter → 4× BPF.
Topology rule 3ChannelSplitter block removed — replaced by 6 per-antenna 1:4 splitters.
Topology rule 424 RF outputs preserve phase/amplitude per antenna per band for DF / monopulse / beamforming.
