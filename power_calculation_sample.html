Power Converters & LDOs — Dissipation and Thermal Analysis

Each regulator's dissipation is P_diss = (V_in − V_out) × I_out for linear LDOs, or P_out × (1 − η) / η for switching converters. Junction temperature is T_j = T_ambient + P_diss × θ_ja evaluated at T_ambient = 85 °C (worst-case avionics chamber), with θ_jc / θ_ja from the datasheet or typical package values.
