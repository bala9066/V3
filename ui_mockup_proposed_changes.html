§2.1 Frequency band & tuning ● 98%

Instantaneous bandwidth: 500 MHz. Tuning range: 2.0–18.0 GHz in 1 MHz steps. Reference oscillator: OCXO, ±0.05 ppm over −40 to +85 °C.

§3.2 System noise figure budget ● 92%

System NF target: ≤3.0 dB at mid-band. LNA gain 18 dB, NF 1.4 dB. Downstream NF contribution from mixer + IF chain: 1.6 dB (Friis-referred). Total: 2.8 dB — 200 mW margin against degradation.

§4.4 EMI / EMC compliance envelope ⚠ 58%

Based on airborne platform class, MIL-STD-461G CE102/RE102 applicability is inferred. User has not confirmed radiated emission limit at 2–18 GHz. RS103 (field intensity 200 V/m) assumed for airborne fire-control adjacency. Requires program office confirmation.

§5.1 Rad-tolerance requirement ⚠ 42%

Placeholder: 100 krad(Si) TID, SEE threshold not specified. Components pre-screened against expected total dose, but neutron fluence and SEL cross-section bounds are not yet captured. Flag for Round 2 elicitation.

§6.2 Power supply & thermal ● 88%

Supply rails: +5 V @ 800 mA, +3.3 V @ 1.2 A, −5 V @ 200 mA. Steady-state dissipation: 7.4 W. Junction margin at +85 °C ambient: 12 °C minimum across all active parts.
